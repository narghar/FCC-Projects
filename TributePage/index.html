The man who made us laugh and cry

Robin McLaurin Williams - American comedian and actor was born on 21st, 1951, in Chicago. Robin studied political science before enrolling at Juilliard School to study theatre. He made his feature starring debut playing the title role in Popeye (1980), directed by Robert Altman. Williams, receive Academy Award nominations for Best Actor in a Leading Role in Good Morning, Vietnam (1987), Dead Poets Society (1989), and Fisher King (1991), before he won an Academy Award for his role in Good Will Hunting (1997). In 2014 Williams committed suicide.
